Evaluation Checklist

Toggle glosses on/off and resize the window to test each approach against these criteria:

  • Hanging indent — first line flush left, continuation lines indented ~1.5em
  • No overlap/collapse — words never stack on top of each other
  • Consistent baselines — glossed and un-glossed words sit at the same height
  • Gloss centering — English text centered below its Greek word
  • Natural wrapping — glosses stay attached to their words across line breaks
  • Paragraph spacing — second paragraph clearly separated from first
  • Glosses-off mode — text collapses cleanly to normal reading layout
